Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix flour, salt, and milk.
  3. Beat in the eggs until smooth.
  4. Pour mixture into a greased baking dish.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden.
